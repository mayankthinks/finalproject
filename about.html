About us

Welcome to
Technical Training Center

Fix Flex has set up skill development and technical training centre known as Technical Training Center (TTC) in urban area that offer Govt. approved courses training in various technical trades.
Our courses include Electrical Wiring, Fan, Washing Machine, Geyser, Cooler, AC, Water Purifier, Fridge, and many other technical trainings through experienced professionals. We are constantly working on updating our training and increasing our training network. Currently our training center are available in bhagalpur and we are coming soon to other major cities in India.

Fix Flex is implementing placement-linked skill development training projects as per CSR partner requirements in the area of technical courses training, employment and Livelihood enhancement which are specifically targeted at underprivileged youth. We create custom offerings that setting up captive Skill Training Center in locations of interest to the corporate, typically urban slums and rural areas, mobilizing aspirants, train them in the courses which have been mutually agreed on and provide them placement into jobs at high-growth companies.

Fix Flex has well-researched industry-aligned course curriculums for over 10 courses across 10 industry sectors. Some of these courses are affiliated and approved by the Bihar State Board of Skill Development (BSBSD), National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC).

What We Do

Technical Education and Skill Training

TTC has set up well-equipped skill development and technical training centers in urban areas and provides govt. approved skills development and technical training in the domain of Electronics, Electrical, Information Technology, etc.

Skill Development Programs

TTC undertake and implement placement linked skill development training projects as per partner requirements in their CSR obligation in the area of education, Employment and Livelihood Enhancement which are specifically targeted at underprivileged youth.

Entrepreneurship Development Programs

If the candidates are willing to do their own business instead of doing the job, TTC provides training to them through its Entrepreneurship Development Program (EDP).

Technical Training Solutions for Industry

TTC offers customized courses according to industry requirements to develop competency and improve the productivity of existing workforces. It’s offering training need analysis, content development, training delivery at the client location, and certification.
