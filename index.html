About us

Welcome to
Technical Training Center

The “Technical Training Center (TTC)” is an initiative of the Fix Flex to deliver technical education and skill development training for aspiring youth, helping them gain employability and entrepreneurship skill and empowering them to pursue a sustainable livelihood in the world of work.

TTC has set up skill development and technical training centers in urban areas that offer Govt. approved courses training in various technical trades.

What 

 We Do

  • Technical Training Center

    TTC has set up well-equipped skill development and technical training center in urban areas and provides govt. approved skills development and technical courses training in the field of Electronics, Electrical, Mechanical, Information Technology, etc.

  • Skill Development Programs

    TTC undertake and implement placement linked skill development training projects as per partner requirements in their CSR obligation in the area of education, Employment and Livelihood Enhancement which are specifically targeted at underprivileged youth.

  • Entrepreneurship Development Programs

    If the candidates are willing to do their own business instead of doing the job, TTC provides training to them through its Entrepreneurship Development Program (EDP).

  • Technical Training Solutions for Industry

    TTC offers customized courses according to industry requirements to develop competency and improve the productivity of existing workforces. It’s offering training need analysis, content development, training delivery at the client location, and certification.
